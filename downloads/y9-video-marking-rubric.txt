Year 9 Video Editing Homework — Marking Rubric (20 marks)

A) Branding (0–4)
- 0: No clear branding
- 1–2: Brand name present but inconsistent/unclear
- 3: Consistent style + brand identity
- 4: Strong brand identity (logo/name, colours/fonts, polished)

B) Product focus (0–4)
- 0: Unclear what is being advertised
- 1–2: Product shown but message is weak
- 3: Clear product focus + key selling point(s)
- 4: Product is the star; benefits are obvious and memorable

C) Persuasion (0–4)
- 0: No persuasive techniques
- 1–2: Some persuasion but generic
- 3: Persuasive language/techniques + clear CTA
- 4: Convincing tone; CTA is strong and well placed

D) Editing & pacing (0–4)
- 0: Poor timing; hard to follow
- 1–2: Basic editing; pacing is uneven
- 3: Good pacing; transitions and timing used well
- 4: Professional feel; excellent timing and readability

E) Audio & finish (0–4)
- 0: No audio or distracting audio
- 1–2: Audio present but unbalanced/doesn’t fit
- 3: Audio supports the advert; balanced
- 4: Audio elevates the advert; clean export; correct length (20–30s)

Notes:
- If length is not 20–30 seconds, cap E at 2 marks unless corrected.
- If share permissions block viewing, mark as “incomplete” until resubmitted link.
