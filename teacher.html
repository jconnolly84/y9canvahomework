Quick rubric (20 marks)

  • Branding (0–4): brand name/logo, consistent style.
  • Product focus (0–4): clear product + key selling point(s).
  • Persuasion (0–4): message, tone, call to action.
  • Editing & pacing (0–4): timing, readability, transitions used well.
  • Audio & finish (0–4): audio choices, polish, correct length (20–30s).
